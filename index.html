About Me


My name is Kristen Fairman and life has taken me in a number of directions. During school I was actively involved in Nordic Skiing which taught me the value of hard work and allowed me to travel the world. I was a member of the US Development Team at an early age and raced at the Division 1 level at Middlebury College in Vermont. While there I studied Geography, Architectural History and Design, and developed a passion for the Russian language. I spent a summer working for the world reknowned language schools at Middlebury during a summer break.

After graduation I moved back to Minnesota where I began working as a certified personal trainer while simultaneously pursuing a Master of Arts in Teaching. After discovering that teaching at the elementary school level was not my calling, I moved to Chicago where I completed my Master of Arts in Forensic Psychology and worked as a Data Analyst for The Chicago School of Professional Psychology. Upon returning to Minnesota, I began a doctoral program in Clinical Psychology and worked in transitional care for adults with co-morbid chemical dependency and mental illness. Finding this work draining, I began to look for other career paths that could combine my passion for language, design, psychology, and creativity. This is where I find myself pursuing coding and web development as my future career.
